About Cube Network

Cube Network is a community-driven Minecraft server built for players who love creativity, competition, and fun. From survival adventures to custom features, our world is designed to give you the ultimate Minecraft experience.

Server & Discord Rules

Respect all players. No hacking. No toxicity. Keep it fun for everyone.

General Rules

  • Be respectful to all players and staff members
  • No griefing, stealing, or destroying other players' builds
  • No hacking, cheating, or use of unauthorized mods
  • Keep chat clean and family-friendly
  • No spamming or excessive caps in chat
  • Follow staff instructions at all times
  • No advertising other servers or Discord channels
  • Use common sense and maintain a positive atmosphere

Discord Rules

  • Keep discussions in appropriate channels
  • No NSFW content or inappropriate usernames
  • Respect voice channel etiquette
  • No excessive pinging or DM harassment
  • Follow Discord Terms of Service

Breaking these rules may result in warnings, mutes, kicks, or permanent bans depending on severity.
